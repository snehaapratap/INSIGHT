Follow the hilarious and relatable escapades of Bridget Jones as she navigates the challenges of modern dating and life while keeping a candid diary.Bridget Jones's Diary is a 1996 novel by Helen Fielding. Written in the form of a personal diary, the novel chronicles a year in the life of Bridget Jones, a thirty-something single working woman living in London. She writes about her career, self-image, vices, family, friends, and romantic relationships.
