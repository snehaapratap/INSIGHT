Author : Sophie Kinsella

"I've Got Your Number" is a delightful romantic comedy novel by Sophie Kinsella. Poppy Wyatt, the protagonist, loses her engagement ring and her phone in quick succession. She finds a discarded phone in a hotel trash can and decides to keep it. The phone belongs to businessman Sam Roxton, who reluctantly agrees to let Poppy use it temporarily. As Poppy juggles wedding planning, a demanding job, and unexpected texts on the borrowed phone, a unique bond forms between her and Sam. The novel humorously explores modern communication, personal growth, and the unpredictable nature of connections as Poppy and Sam navigate misunderstandings, secrets, and a budding romance.
