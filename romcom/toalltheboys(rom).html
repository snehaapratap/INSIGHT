Author : Jenny Han

"To All the Boys I've Loved Before" is a charming young adult novel written by Jenny Han. The story follows Lara Jean Covey, a shy and introspective teenager who writes secret love letters to all her past crushes, never intending to send them. However, her life takes an unexpected turn when the letters are accidentally mailed out, leading to a series of romantic and often humorous situations. As Lara Jean navigates the consequences of her secret being exposed, the book delves into themes of self-discovery, first love, and the complexities of relationships, making it a heartwarming and relatable coming-of-age tale.
