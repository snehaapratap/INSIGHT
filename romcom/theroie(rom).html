Author : Graeme Simsion

"The Rosie Project" is a charming and heartwarming novel by Graeme Simsion. It follows the story of Don Tillman, a socially awkward genetics professor who embarks on a unique journey to find a suitable life partner. Don, with his meticulous and scientific approach to life, creates a detailed questionnaire he calls the "Wife Project" to find his perfect match. However, his plans take an unexpected turn when he meets Rosie, a free-spirited bartender who doesn't fit his criteria but ignites a series of humorous and touching escapades. The book explores themes of love, acceptance, and the beauty of human imperfections, delivering a delightful blend of romance and humor.
