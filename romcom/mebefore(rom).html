Author : Jojo Moyes

"Me Before You" by Jojo Moyes is a poignant and thought-provoking novel that delves into the profound relationship between Louisa Clark and Will Traynor. Louisa, a cheerful and quirky young woman, becomes a caregiver to Will, a former successful businessman left paralyzed after an accident. As their connection deepens, Louisa discovers Will's desire to end his life due to his changed circumstances. The book navigates themes of love, life-altering choices, and the importance of understanding and compassion. With its emotional exploration and compelling characters, "Me Before You" is a touching and unforgettable tale that prompts reflection on the value of life and personal autonomy.
