Author : Sphie Kinsella

"Can You Keep a Secret?" by Sophie Kinsella is a lighthearted and entertaining novel that follows the misadventures of Emma Corrigan. During a turbulent plane ride, Emma spills her deepest secrets to a stranger, thinking they'll never meet again. However, that stranger turns out to be her company's enigmatic CEO, Jack Harper. As her professional and personal worlds collide, Emma's attempts to keep her secrets hidden lead to a series of hilarious and endearing situations. The book explores themes of honesty, vulnerability, and unexpected connections, all wrapped up in a delightful romantic comedy that keeps readers engaged and smiling.
