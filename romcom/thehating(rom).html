Author : Sally Thorne

The Hating Game" by Sally Thorne is a captivating contemporary romance novel that follows the dynamic between Lucy Hutton and Joshua Templeman, coworkers at a publishing house. The two engage in a fierce rivalry, engaging in a playful yet intense battle of wits and pranks. As their interactions evolve, the lines between love and hate blur, leading to unexpected feelings. The book skillfully combines witty banter, palpable chemistry, and character-driven storytelling, creating a rollercoaster of emotions as Lucy and Joshua navigate their complex emotions and vulnerabilities. "The Hating Game" is a delightful exploration of office dynamics and the thin line between animosity and attraction.
