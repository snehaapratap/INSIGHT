Author : Beth O'Leary

"The Flatshare" by Beth O'Leary is a heartwarming contemporary romance novel centered around an unconventional living arrangement. Tiffy and Leon share a flat, but they've never met in person. Tiffy occupies the apartment during the evenings, while Leon uses it during the day. They communicate through post-it notes and gradually form a deep connection despite their physical distance. As they exchange notes about their lives, hopes, and fears, their relationship evolves into something unexpected and beautiful. The book explores themes of healing from past trauma, the power of human connection, and the transformative nature of love in an utterly charming and unique way.
