Author : Jasmine Guillory

"The Wedding Date" by Jasmine Guillory is a delightful contemporary romance novel that starts with a chance encounter. Alexa Monroe and Drew Nichols meet in a stuck elevator, and when Drew is in need of a plus-one for a wedding, he impulsively asks Alexa to accompany him. Their pretend date leads to genuine sparks, and they embark on a long-distance relationship despite their busy lives and career commitments. The novel beautifully explores themes of vulnerability, interracial relationships, and the challenges of modern dating. With its engaging writing and relatable characters, "The Wedding Date" delivers a heartwarming and engaging tale of unexpected love.
