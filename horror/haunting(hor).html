HORROR

Author : Mary Shelley

Shirley Jackson's unsettling tale follows four individuals brought together to explore the supernatural phenomena of Hill House. The house's oppressive atmosphere and eerie occurrences intensify their fears and vulnerabilities, leaving readers to question whether it's the house that's haunted or their own psyches. The novel examines psychological fragility, isolation, and the blurred lines between reality and the supernatural.
