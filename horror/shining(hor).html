HORROR

Author : Stephen King

In this psychological horror, the Torrance family becomes winter caretakers of the remote Overlook Hotel. As isolation and supernatural forces take hold, Jack Torrance's descent into madness threatens his wife and psychic son. Stephen King masterfully explores the power of the human psyche, haunted pasts, and the chilling reality that some places can be as malevolent as the people within them.
