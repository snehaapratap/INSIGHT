HORROR

Author : Bram Stoker

In this iconic Gothic novel, Count Dracula, a centuries-old vampire, leaves his Transylvanian castle to spread his dark influence in London. The novel weaves a tale of suspense and horror as Dracula's sinister intentions clash with the efforts of a group of characters to stop his reign of terror, exploring themes of fear, lust, and the eternal struggle between good and evil.
