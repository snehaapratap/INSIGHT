HORROR

Author : Mark Z.Danielewski

A labyrinthine narrative follows a family's experience in a house that defies the laws of physics, with rooms and passages appearing out of nowhere. As they document their ordeal, a man named Johnny Truant becomes obsessed with their story. This unconventional horror novel blurs reality, weaving multiple narratives, typography shifts, and footnotes, delving into the terror of the unknown and the limits of human comprehension.
