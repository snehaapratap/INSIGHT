HORROR

Author : Josh Malerman

In a post-apocalyptic world plagued by mysterious creatures that drive people to madness upon sight, Malorie must navigate a river blindfolded to save her children. This suspenseful thriller explores fear of the unknown, the lengths to which one will go to protect loved ones, and the psychological toll of a world where visibility means death.
