HORROR

Author : Thomas Harris

FBI trainee Clarice Starling seeks the help of the incarcerated, brilliant psychiatrist and cannibalistic serial killer, Dr. Hannibal Lecter, to catch another serial killer known as Buffalo Bill. Thomas Harris crafts a tense narrative of psychological manipulation, moral ambiguity, and the hunt for a depraved killer, with Lecter's chilling intellect at the heart of the story.
