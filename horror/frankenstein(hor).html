HORROR

Author : Mary Shelley

Mary Shelley's groundbreaking work introduces Victor Frankenstein, a scientist who creates a creature through unconventional means. As the creature's journey unfolds, it grapples with its existence, yearning for acceptance while facing rejection and isolation. This novel delves into ethical dilemmas, the consequences of unchecked ambition, and the blurred line between creator and creation.
