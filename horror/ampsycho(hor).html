HORROR

Author : Bret Easton Ellis

Bret Easton Ellis presents a disturbing exploration of consumerism, vanity, and psychopathy through the lens of Patrick Bateman, a wealthy Manhattan investment banker who leads a double life as a sadistic serial killer. The novel's graphic violence and dark humor underscore its critique of excess, conformity, and the dehumanizing effects of modern society.
