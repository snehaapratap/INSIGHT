HORROR

Author : William Peter Blatty

When a young girl becomes possessed by a demonic entity, her mother seeks the help of two priests to perform an exorcism. William Peter Blatty's novel delves into themes of faith, the battle between good and evil, and the fragility of the human spirit. It's a chilling exploration of supernatural forces and the lengths to which humanity will go to confront them.
