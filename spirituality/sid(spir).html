SPIRITUALITY

Author : Hermann Hesse

Hermann Hesse's classic novel follows Siddhartha, a young man on a quest for spiritual enlightenment. Set against the backdrop of ancient India, the book explores Siddhartha's encounters with various philosophies and his ultimate realization that true wisdom comes from personal experience and inner understanding.
