SPIRITUALITY

Author : Don Miguel Ruiz

Don Miguel Ruiz presents ancient Toltec wisdom in this concise guide to personal freedom and transformation. The book introduces four simple yet powerful agreements—be impeccable with your word, don't take anything personally, don't make assumptions, and always do your best—which, when practiced, can lead to a life of fulfillment and harmony.
