Author : Charles Duhigg

"The Power of Habit" is a book written by Charles Duhigg, published in 2012. It explores the science behind habits and their role in our lives. The book delves into how habits work, why they form, and how they can be changed. Duhigg presents real-life examples from various contexts, including personal, organizational, and societal, to illustrate the power of habits and how they can be harnessed to achieve personal and professional transformation. The book also highlights the neurological and psychological processes that underlie habit formation and change, providing insights into how habits can be manipulated for positive outcomes.
