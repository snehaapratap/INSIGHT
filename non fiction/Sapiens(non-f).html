Author : Yual Noah Harari

"Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind" is a non-fiction book written by Yuval Noah Harari. Published in 2014, the book explores the history of our species, Homo sapiens, from its earliest days to the present. Harari covers a wide range of topics, including the cognitive revolution, agricultural revolution, rise of empires, and the impact of technology on human societies. The book provides a thought-provoking overview of how Homo sapiens became the dominant species on Earth and examines the cultural, social, and technological developments that shaped our world.
