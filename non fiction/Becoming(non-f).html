Author : Michelle Obama

"Becoming" is a memoir written by Michelle Obama, the former First Lady of the United States. Published in 2018, the book provides a candid and personal account of her life, from her childhood and early experiences to her time in the White House. It delves into her upbringing, education, career, and the challenges she faced along the way. The book also explores her roles as a wife, mother, and advocate for various causes, offering insights into her journey of self-discovery and growth.
