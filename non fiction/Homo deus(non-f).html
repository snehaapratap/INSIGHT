Author : Yual Noah Harari

"Homo Deus: A Brief History of Tomorrow" is a book written by Yuval Noah Harari, an Israeli historian. Published in 2015, the book explores the potential future of humankind, focusing on the intersections of technology, biology, and society. Harari delves into how advancements in fields like artificial intelligence, biotechnology, and data science might shape the trajectory of human evolution and existence in the coming decades. The book contemplates the challenges and ethical dilemmas that could arise as humans gain unprecedented control over their own biology and the world around them.
