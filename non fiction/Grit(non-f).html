Author : Angela Duckworth

"Grit: The Power of Passion and Perseverance" is a popular psychology book written by Angela Duckworth. Published in 2016, the book explores the concept of grit, which Duckworth defines as a combination of passion and perseverance toward long-term goals. She argues that talent alone isn't enough for achieving success, and that the dedication to continuously work hard and overcome challenges is a key factor. Duckworth presents research findings and real-life examples to support her ideas, highlighting the importance of effort and determination in achieving one's ambitions. The book has gained attention in the fields of education, business, and personal development for its insights into achievement and resilience.
