MYTHOLOGY

Author : Neil Gaiman

"Norse Mythology" is a book written by Neil Gaiman that was published in 2017. It's a retelling of various myths from Norse mythology, featuring gods like Odin, Thor, and Loki, along with their adventures, conflicts, and interactions with other mythical beings. Gaiman draws inspiration from the Poetic Edda and the Prose Edda, two primary sources of Norse mythology, to craft a modern and accessible narrative. The book explores the creation of the world, the realms of the gods, and the events leading up to Ragnarok, the ultimate battle and downfall of the gods.
