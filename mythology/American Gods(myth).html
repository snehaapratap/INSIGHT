MYTHOLOGY

Author : Neil Gaiman

"American Gods" is a fantasy novel written by Neil Gaiman, first published in 2001. The story follows Shadow Moon, a man who gets released from prison early due to his wife's sudden death. He becomes entangled in a conflict between old gods who have lost their power and new gods that represent modern concepts like media, technology, and globalization. As Shadow navigates this mysterious world, he encounters various mythological figures and explores the concept of belief in a changing world. The novel delves into themes of mythology, cultural identity, and the clash between tradition and modernity. It has been praised for its unique storytelling, blending of different genres, and thought-provoking ideas.
