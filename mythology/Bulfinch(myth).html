MYTHOLOGY

Author : Thomas Bulfinch

"Bulfinch's Mythology" is a collection of myths and legends from various cultures, compiled and written by Thomas Bulfinch. First published in the 19th century, the book presents stories from Greek, Roman, Norse, and other mythologies, providing readers with an overview of ancient tales about gods, heroes, and supernatural beings. The work has become a classic reference for those interested in exploring the world of mythology and its cultural significance.
