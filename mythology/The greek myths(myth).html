MYTHOLOGY

Author : Robert Graves

"The Greek Myths" is a comprehensive and influential work written by Robert Graves. Originally published in 1955, the book delves into the world of Greek mythology, presenting a compilation of ancient stories, gods, heroes, and legends from Greek culture. Graves offers his interpretations and analyses of these myths, exploring their origins, symbolism, and significance in a literary and historical context. The book has been praised for its detailed retelling of myths and its unique perspectives on their meanings. Keep in mind that while the book is a valuable resource, it's important to consider that Graves' interpretations can be subjective and may differ from other scholarly viewpoints.
