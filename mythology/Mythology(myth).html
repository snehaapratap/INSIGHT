MYTHOLOGY

Author : Edith Hamilton

"Mythology" is a classic book written by Edith Hamilton. Published in 1942, the book provides an overview of Greek, Roman, and Norse mythologies, delving into the stories of gods, heroes, and legendary figures. Edith Hamilton's engaging writing style and comprehensive approach have made this book a popular choice for those interested in exploring ancient myths and their cultural significance.
