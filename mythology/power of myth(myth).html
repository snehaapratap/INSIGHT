MYTHOLOGY

Author : Joseph Campbell

"The Power of Myth" is a book by Joseph Campbell, originally published in 1988. It's based on a series of conversations between Campbell and journalist Bill Moyers. The book delves into the role of myths in human culture and explores how myths shape our understanding of the world, our beliefs, and our identities. Campbell discusses various mythologies from different cultures and highlights the universal themes that connect them. The book is known for its insights into the human experience and its exploration of the enduring power of storytelling.
