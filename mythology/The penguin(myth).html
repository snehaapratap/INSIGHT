MYTHOLOGY

Author : Jenny March

"The Penguin Book of Classical Myths" is a comprehensive collection of Greek and Roman myths compiled and written by Jenny March. It offers readers an overview of the rich mythological traditions of ancient Greece and Rome, featuring stories of gods, heroes, and legendary creatures. The book provides insight into the cultural and historical significance of these myths while presenting them in an engaging and accessible manner. It's a valuable resource for anyone interested in exploring the fascinating world of classical mythology.
