MYTHOLOGY

Author : Stephen Fry

"Mythos" is a book written by British author Stephen Fry. It was published in 2017 and explores various Greek myths, retelling them in Fry's distinctive and engaging narrative style. The book covers the stories of gods, goddesses, heroes, and monsters from Greek mythology, providing a modern and humorous take on these timeless tales. It's a great read for anyone interested in mythology and ancient stories.
