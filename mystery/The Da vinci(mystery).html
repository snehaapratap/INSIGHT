MYSTERY

Author : Dan Brown

"The Da Vinci Code" is a mystery thriller novel written by Dan Brown. It was published in 2003 and quickly became a bestseller. The story revolves around Robert Langdon, a Harvard professor of symbology, who is drawn into a complex web of conspiracy and symbolism after a murder at the Louvre Museum in Paris. The book delves into religious and historical themes, exploring theories about the Holy Grail, the role of Mary Magdalene, and the influence of secret societies like the Priory of Sion. The novel's blend of art, history, and intrigue has captivated readers around the world.
