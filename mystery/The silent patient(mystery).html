MYSTERY

Author : Alex Michaelides

"The Silent Patient" is a psychological thriller novel written by Alex Michaelides. It was published in 2019. The story revolves around Alicia Berenson, a famous painter who shoots her husband and then stops speaking. She is institutionalized at a psychiatric facility, where psychotherapist Theo Faber becomes intrigued by her case and is determined to unravel the mystery behind her silence. The book delves into themes of psychotherapy, trauma, and the complexities of human psychology. It gained widespread popularity for its suspenseful plot and unexpected twists.
