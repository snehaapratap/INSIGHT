MYSTERY

Author : Gillian Flynn

"Gone Girl" is a psychological thriller novel written by Gillian Flynn, first published in 2012. The story revolves around the mysterious disappearance of Amy Dunne on her fifth wedding anniversary. The novel delves into themes of marriage, deceit, manipulation, and the media's influence on public perception. Told from alternating perspectives of husband Nick and wife Amy, the narrative unravels a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected twists. The book's exploration of unreliable narrators and complex characters has made it a popular and critically acclaimed work in the thriller genre. It was also adapted into a successful film in 2014.
