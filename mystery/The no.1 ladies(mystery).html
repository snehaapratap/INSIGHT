Author : Alxander McCall

"The No. 1 Ladies' Detective Agency" is a novel written by Alexander McCall Smith. It was first published in 1998. Set in Botswana, the book follows the story of Mma Precious Ramotswe, the founder of the first female-owned detective agency in the country. Precious Ramotswe uses her intelligence, compassion, and intuition to solve various cases, ranging from missing persons to small mysteries. The novel combines elements of mystery, humor, and African culture, and it is the first installment in a series of novels featuring Mma Ramotswe's adventures.
