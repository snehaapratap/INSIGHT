MYSTERY

Author : Donna Tartt

"The Secret History" is a novel written by Donna Tartt. It was first published in 1992. The story revolves around a group of students at an elite college in New England who become entangled in a murder they committed. The book explores themes of friendship, guilt, and the consequences of one's actions. It's known for its dark and atmospheric storytelling, as well as its exploration of the psychological and moral complexities of its characters.
