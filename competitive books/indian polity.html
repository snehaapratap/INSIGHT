Laxmikanth is an authoritative and widely acclaimed book for individuals preparing for competitive exams like the UPSC (Union Public Service Commission) Civil Services Examination. "Indian Polity" is a comprehensive and well-structured textbook authored by Mr. M. Laxmikanth, an esteemed academician and expert in the field of Indian politics and governance. This book has established itself as a trusted resource for UPSC aspirants and is widely regarded for its clarity, depth, and extensive coverage of the Indian political system.
