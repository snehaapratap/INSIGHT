COMPETITIVE

Author :Rajiv Vijay

"BIOLOGY FOR NEET" is a popular and highly useful book for students preparing for competitive exams like NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) in India. This book, often referred to as "BIO FOR NEET," is designed to complement the NCERT (National Council of Educational Research and Training) textbooks and provide a comprehensive and concise overview of the NEET syllabus. It is a game-changing book in the field of competitive exam preparation. It's a one-stop solution for NEET aspirants who are looking to master the essential topics covered in the NCERT textbooks, which form the basis of the NEET examination.
