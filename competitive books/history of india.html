Author :Bipin Chandra

The book "History of Modern India" by Bipin Chandra is a seminal work in the field of Indian history and a highly regarded resource for students preparing for competitive exams, especially the UPSC (Union Public Service Commission) Civil Services Examination. Authored by the eminent historian Bipin Chandra, this book is an essential companion for those seeking a comprehensive understanding of modern Indian history. "History of Modern India" is a definitive and meticulously researched textbook that delves into the historical narrative of India, starting from the decline of the Mughal Empire to the struggle for independence and the formation of the modern Indian nation-state. This book is renowned for its lucid narrative, deep insights, and extensive coverage of significant events and personalities that have shaped India's history.
