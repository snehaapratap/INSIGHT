Here's an introduction to an essential book in this context: "Organic Chemistry for NEET" is a comprehensive and dedicated textbook tailored to the specific needs of NEET aspirants. Authored by experienced educators and experts in organic chemistry, this book is a trusted resource for mastering the intricacies of this subject.
