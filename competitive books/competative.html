Author : H C VERMA

"Concepts of Physics" by H.C. Verma is a highly regarded and widely used textbook for students preparing for competitive exams like NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) in India. This book, authored by Dr. Harish Chandra Verma, is renowned for its comprehensive coverage of physics concepts, clear explanations, and an abundance of solved examples and practice problems.Concepts of Physics" is not just a book; it's a trusted companion for students aspiring to excel in physics, particularly when preparing for medical and engineering entrance exams. Dr. H.C. Verma, a distinguished physicist and educator, has crafted this book to provide a solid foundation in physics principles and problem-solving skills.
